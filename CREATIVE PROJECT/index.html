SUNNY:

Sunny weather days are days when there are very little or no clouds in the sky. We usually experience more sunny days in the summer when the days are warmer.

CLOUDY:

The weather is cloudy when there are lots of clouds in the sky that get in the way of the sun. Cloudy days can be cold, because there's not much sunlight, but they can also be warm if there is lots of humidity.

RAINY:

A rainy day is a day when the sky is filled with clouds and drops of water fall from them. Some people like rainy days because they feel cozy and relaxed, while others dislike them because they feel gloomy and wet.
